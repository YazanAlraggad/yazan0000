Syrup:

Syrup preservative :

1- Ascorbic acid
2- Benzoic acid
3- Propyl paraben

Add preservatives according to the percentage of sucrose :

1- Less 85% need preservatives
2- 85% not need preservatives
3-Up 85% crystallization .

Experiment ( 11 ) : Simple syrup (85%)

Don't need preservatives

RX:
sucrose 5 gm
Water up to 100 ml

Method of preparation :

1- Weight 85 gm sucrose and put it in beaker on water bath with 60 ml water until dissolve .
2-put 40 ml water in another beaker on water bath .
3-when sucrose dissolved add the remaining amount of water .

Uses :

1- Sweet vehicle
2- Sweeting agent .
