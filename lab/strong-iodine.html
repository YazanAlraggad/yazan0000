Potassium permanganate

Strong Iodine Solution ( 5% )

RX :
I2 5g
KI 10g
Water up to 100 ml

Method of preparation :

1- Weight 10 g of KI and dissolve in 60 ml water in flask then add 5g of I2 and shaking.
2 – -Transfer it to graduated cylinder and complete the volume to 100 ml .

Uses

1-Hypothyroidism ( internal use )
2- disinfectant , antiseptic , antifungal ( external use ) .
