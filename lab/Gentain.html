Experiment (2) :
Gentian Violet

Crystal Violet (1%)

RX :
Gentian Violet 1 g
Ethanol 90% 10ml
Water up to 100 ml

Method of preparation :

1 - Dissolve 1g of gentian violet in 10 ml of ethanol in flask .
2 - Add the solution in flask with 60 ml of water and shaking .
3 - Transfer it to graduated cylinder and complete the volume to 100 ml .

Uses

1-Dermatological condition .
2- Antiseptic for burns and wound
