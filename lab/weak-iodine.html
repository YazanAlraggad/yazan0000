Experiment (3 ) :
Potassium permanganate

Weak Iodine Solution ( 2% )

RX :
I2 2g
KI 2.5g
Water up to 100 ml

Method of preparation :

1- Weight 2.5 g of KI and dissolve in 60 ml water in flask then add 2g of I2 and shaking.
2 – -Transfer it to graduated cylinder and complete the volume to 100 ml .

Uses

Antiseptic for wound and burns .
