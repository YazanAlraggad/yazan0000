Experiment (1 ) :
Potassium permanganate

Strong oxidizing agent (1%)

RX :
KMNO4 0.1 g
Water up to 100 ml

Method of preparation :

1 - weight 0.1 g of KMNO4
2 – Dissolve 0.1 g of KMNO4 in 80ml of water in flask.
3 – Shake to dissolve then transfer it to graduated cylinder and complete the volume to 100 ml

Uses

1- Eczema
2- Wound cleansing
3- Athlete`s Foot
