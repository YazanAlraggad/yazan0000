Experiment (5) : Normal saline solution (0.9% ) :

( Isotonic solution)

RX :
NaCl 0.9 g
Water up to 100 ml

Method of preparation :

1- Weight 0.9 g of NaCl and put in flask .
2 - Dissolve in 60 ml of water and shaking .
3- Transfer it to graduated cylinder and complete the volume to 100 ml .

Uses

1- Dehydration
2- Clean Wounds
3- Cleansing contact lenses
4- Nasal irrigation

(White / Red label )
