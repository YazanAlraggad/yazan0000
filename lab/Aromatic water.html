Aromatic water :-

Properties should be present in aromatic water :

1- Clear without any impurity .
2- Colorless
3- It is taste and small refers to the aromatic substances that was used .

NOTE

dUses of aromatic water :

1- Expectorant ______ camphor water
2-Carmminative _______peppermint , chloroform water
3-anti spasmodic ______peppermint water
4-Flavoring agent_______Rose water

Experiment ( 9 ) : Chloroform water

RX:
Chloroform 0,5 ml
Water up to 100ml

Uses :

1- carminative
2- expectorant

(White label )

NOTE

When chloroform exposed to air and heat it well change to a phasogen ( toxic form )

Experiment ( 10 ) : Peppermint water

RX:-
Peppermint oil 20 ml
Ethanol 90% 600 ml
Water up to 1000 ml

Method of preparation :

1- Dissolved peppermint oil in ethanol then add water gradually .
2- Add Talc and shaking .
3- Used filter paper to filtration solution .

Uses :

Anti spasmodic 15 ml three time daily .

(White label)

NOTE

Talc use as a dispersing agent .
