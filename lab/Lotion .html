Experiment (15) : Calamine lotion

Rx
Calamine 80gm
Zinc oxide 80gm
Glycerin 20ml
Bentonite magma 250ml

Method of preparation :

1- Dilute the bentonite magma with lime Ca(OH)2 in an equal amount .
2- Weight calamine , zinc oxide , glycerin and put mortar.
3- Add the solution which you dilution .
4- Transfer it to graduated cylinder and complete the volume by Ca(OH)2 .
